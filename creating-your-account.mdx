Creating a DIYHomie account takes under two minutes and unlocks the full power of the app — saved projects, progress that follows you across devices, and recommendations tailored to the projects you've worked on before. You can sign up directly inside the mobile app or through the web at [app.diyhomie.com](https://app.diyhomie.com). Choose your platform below for step-by-step instructions.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Mobile App">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download and open the app">
        Install DIYHomie from the **App Store** (iOS) or **Google Play** (Android), then open the app. You'll land on the welcome screen.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Get Started">
        Tap the **Get Started** button at the bottom of the welcome screen to open the sign-up flow.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose your sign-in method">
        Select how you'd like to create your account:

        - **Email** — Enter your email address and create a password (at least 8 characters).
        - **Continue with Google** — Tap this option and choose your Google account in the prompt that appears.
        - **Continue with Apple** — Tap this option and authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password. You can choose to hide your email using Apple's private relay.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm your email (email sign-up only)">
        If you signed up with email, check your inbox for a confirmation message from DIYHomie and tap the **Verify Email** link inside it. Once confirmed, you're taken directly into the app.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete your profile">
        Tell DIYHomie a little about yourself — your experience level (beginner, intermediate, or advanced) and the type of home you have (house, apartment, condo). This takes about 30 seconds and helps the app surface the most relevant projects for you right away.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web Browser">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to app.diyhomie.com">
        Open any modern browser and navigate to [app.diyhomie.com](https://app.diyhomie.com). The DIYHomie welcome page loads with sign-in and sign-up options.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click Create Account">
        Click the **Create Account** button in the center of the page or in the top-right navigation bar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose your sign-in method">
        Select how you'd like to register:

        - **Email** — Enter your email address and create a password (at least 8 characters), then click **Continue**.
        - **Continue with Google** — Click this button and complete the Google OAuth prompt in the pop-up window.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm your email (email sign-up only)">
        Check your inbox for a verification email from DIYHomie. Click the **Verify Email** button inside it. You'll be redirected back to the app and signed in automatically.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete your profile">
        Answer a few quick questions about your experience level and home type so DIYHomie can personalize your Home Feed from day one. You can update these preferences any time in **Profile & Settings**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What your account unlocks

Once you're signed in, your DIYHomie account gives you access to features that make every project smoother:

- **Saved Projects** — Bookmark any guide to find it again instantly, even offline.
- **Progress Tracking** — Step completions are saved automatically so you can stop mid-project and pick up exactly where you left off, on any device.
- **Personalized Recommendations** — The more projects you browse and complete, the better DIYHomie understands the types of tasks you work on and surfaces relevant guides on your Home Feed.
- **Project History** — Review every project you've started or completed, including notes and photos you added along the way.
- **AI Assistant Memory** — The assistant remembers context within a conversation so you can ask follow-up questions naturally without repeating yourself.

<Note>
  DIYHomie is free to use for all core features including step-by-step guides, the AI assistant, and progress tracking. A **DIYHomie Pro** subscription unlocks offline access to all guides, advanced AI features, and ad-free browsing. You can start a free trial from **Profile & Settings** at any time.
</Note>

## Account help

<Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
  If you signed up with email and can't remember your password:

  1. On the sign-in screen, tap or click **Forgot password?** below the password field.
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account and tap **Send Reset Link**.
  3. Check your inbox for an email from DIYHomie with the subject line "Reset your password."
  4. Click the **Reset Password** link inside the email — it's valid for 30 minutes.
  5. Enter and confirm your new password, then click **Save**. You'll be signed in automatically.

  If you don't see the reset email within a few minutes, check your spam folder. If you signed up using Google or Apple, you don't have a DIYHomie password — use the same sign-in button you used when you created your account.
</Accordion>
