DIYHomie is free to get started — you can browse projects, follow step-by-step guides, and ask the AI assistant a limited number of questions each month without spending a thing. For homeowners who want unlimited AI access, the full project library, cost estimation tools, and offline support, the Premium plan unlocks everything the app has to offer. Here's how the two plans compare and how to manage your subscription.

## Free vs. Premium

<CardGroup cols={2}>

## How to Upgrade to Premium

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap the **gear icon** on the profile tab (mobile) or click your profile picture in the top-right corner and select **Account Settings** (web).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Upgrade to Premium">
    Select **Subscription** from the settings menu, then tap **Upgrade to Premium**. You'll see the current pricing for monthly and annual plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your billing cycle">
    Select **Monthly** or **Annual**. The annual plan offers a significant discount compared to paying month-to-month — the current savings are displayed on the upgrade screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete purchase">
    - **iOS:** Confirm purchase through Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password. Your subscription is billed through the App Store.
    - **Android:** Confirm through Google Play using your Google account payment method.
    - **Web:** Enter your payment details on the secure checkout page powered by Stripe.

    Premium features activate immediately after purchase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Billing

- **Monthly plan:** billed on the same date each month; auto-renews until cancelled
- **Annual plan:** billed once per year; auto-renews on the anniversary date
- You'll receive an email receipt for each billing cycle
- To update your payment method, go to **Settings** → **Subscription** → **Manage Billing** (web) or update your payment method through your app store account settings (iOS/Android)

## How to Cancel Your Subscription

<Accordion title="Cancel on iOS (App Store)">
  1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top, then tap **Subscriptions**
  3. Find **DIYHomie** in the list and tap it
  4. Tap **Cancel Subscription** and confirm

  Your Premium access continues until the end of the current billing period. Apple does not automatically issue refunds for unused time — see Apple's refund policy for options.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cancel on Android (Google Play)">
  1. Open the **Google Play Store** app
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top-right corner
  3. Go to **Payments & subscriptions** → **Subscriptions**
  4. Find **DIYHomie** and tap **Cancel subscription**
  5. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation

  Your Premium access continues until the end of the current billing period.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cancel on Web">
  1. Log in to DIYHomie on the web and go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
  2. Tap **Manage Subscription**
  3. Select **Cancel Plan**
  4. Choose a cancellation reason (optional) and confirm

  Your Premium access continues until the end of your current paid period. No further charges will be made after cancellation.
</Accordion>

## Refund Policy

Refunds for DIYHomie Premium are handled by the platform through which you subscribed:

- **iOS:** Refund requests are managed by Apple. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) to request a refund.
- **Android:** Refund requests are managed by Google Play. Visit the [Google Play Help Center](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637) for eligibility and steps.
- **Web:** Contact DIYHomie support within 7 days of your billing date to request a refund for web-based subscriptions. Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

<Note>
  Some features — including billing management options and the exact steps for purchasing — may vary slightly depending on which platform you're using, due to App Store and Google Play policies. If you run into any discrepancies, contact DIYHomie support and we'll help you get sorted.
</Note>
