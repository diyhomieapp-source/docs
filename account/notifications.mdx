DIYHomie sends notifications to help you stay on top of your projects, catch maintenance tasks before they become problems, and hear back from the AI assistant when you're working asynchronously. But everyone's relationship with notifications is different — you might want project reminders and nothing else, or you might want seasonal tips turned on and promotional emails turned off. This page explains every notification type and walks you through exactly how to control each one.

## Notification Types

### Project Reminders
Sent when you haven't opened an in-progress project for a while. These nudges help you pick up where you left off instead of leaving a half-finished project on pause. You can set the frequency (daily, weekly, or never) in notification settings.

### New Project Alerts
Notifies you when DIYHomie adds new guides in project categories you follow — for example, Flooring, Plumbing, or Outdoor & Landscaping. Follow categories from the **Explore** tab to start receiving these alerts.

### Seasonal Maintenance Tips
Timely reminders tied to the current season in your region — things like winterizing outdoor faucets in the fall or checking your AC filter in the spring. These are triggered by your zip code and calendar date, and they're designed to prompt action before a small maintenance task becomes a costly repair.

### AI Conversation Replies
If you ask the AI assistant a question and close the app before it responds (or if you're on a slower connection), DIYHomie can send you a push notification or in-app alert when your answer is ready. This setting only applies when a response is delayed.

### Promotional and App Updates
Occasional messages about new features, limited-time offers, and major app updates. These are sent via email and, optionally, push notification. You can opt out of promotional messages without affecting any other notification type.

## How to Manage Notifications

### In the App
To adjust notification preferences within DIYHomie:

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon on mobile, top-right menu on web)
2. Tap or click **Notifications**
3. Toggle each notification type on or off individually

### Email Preferences
To manage email notifications, go to **Settings** → **Account** → **Email Preferences**. You can toggle individual email types or click the **Unsubscribe** link at the bottom of any DIYHomie email to manage your preferences from your inbox.

### Push Notifications (OS-Level)
If you've turned off push notifications at the system level, in-app toggles won't re-enable them. To restore push notifications:

- **iPhone/iPad:** Go to **Settings** → scroll to **DIYHomie** → tap **Notifications** → turn on **Allow Notifications**
- **Android:** Go to **Settings** → **Apps** → **DIYHomie** → **Notifications** → enable the notification categories you want

## Turning Off Specific Notification Types

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap the **gear icon** on the profile tab (mobile) or click the settings menu in the top-right corner (web).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Notifications">
    Tap **Notifications** to open your full notification preferences panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the notification type you want to change">
    Each notification type is listed with a toggle. Active notifications show a filled toggle; inactive ones are greyed out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle the notification off">
    Tap or click the toggle next to the notification type you want to disable. The change saves automatically — no need to tap a save button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Turn on **Seasonal Maintenance Tips** if you haven't already. These reminders are timed to prompt action at exactly the right point in the year — before the first freeze, before peak humidity season, before heavy rain. Catching maintenance tasks early is almost always cheaper than fixing the damage they cause when ignored.
</Tip>
