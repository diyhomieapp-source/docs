Getting started with DIYHomie takes less time than a trip to the hardware store. In a few quick steps you'll have the app open, your account set up, and your first project guide loaded — whether you're fixing something today or planning ahead for the weekend. Follow the steps below and you'll be ready to tackle any project with confidence.

<Note>
  You don't need any prior home improvement experience to use DIYHomie. Every guide is written for real homeowners, not contractors — no assumed knowledge, no jargon without explanation.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download or open the app">
    DIYHomie is available on every platform:

    - **iOS** — Search "DIYHomie" in the App Store and tap **Get**.
    - **Android** — Search "DIYHomie" in the Google Play Store and tap **Install**.
    - **Web** — Go to [app.diyhomie.com](https://app.diyhomie.com) in any modern browser. No download needed.

    All three platforms share the same account and sync your project progress automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Once the app opens, tap **Get Started** on the welcome screen. You can sign up with:

    - Your **email address and a password**
    - **Continue with Google**
    - **Continue with Apple** (iOS only)

    Your account is free to create. See [Create Your Account](/creating-your-account) for a full walkthrough on each platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse or search for your first project">
    After signing in, you land on your personalized Home Feed. From here you can:

    - **Search** by typing a symptom, room, or task in the search bar at the top (for example: "leaky faucet", "kitchen", or "install dimmer switch").
    - **Browse categories** by tapping a room or project type on the Home Feed.
    - **Ask the AI assistant** to recommend a guide based on what you describe.

    Select any project to open its guide page and review the overview, estimated time, difficulty level, and required tools before committing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the step-by-step guide">
    Inside a project guide, work through each numbered step in order. Each step includes:

    - Written instructions in plain language
    - Photos or diagrams where helpful
    - A checkbox you can tap to mark the step complete

    You can leave and return to the guide any time — DIYHomie saves your progress automatically so you pick up exactly where you left off.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask the AI assistant if you get stuck">
    If anything is unclear or something unexpected comes up, tap the **chat bubble icon** visible on any screen to open the AI assistant. Describe what you're seeing or what went wrong, and the assistant will give you targeted advice for your exact situation — no need to search for a new guide or start over.
  </Step>
</Steps>
