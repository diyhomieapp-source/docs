The DIYHomie project library puts hundreds of guided home improvement projects at your fingertips. Whether you're tackling a weekend renovation or a quick afternoon fix, you can search, filter, and browse to find exactly what you need — no experience required to get started.

## Search the Project Library

The search bar at the top of the **Projects** tab is the fastest way to find what you're looking for. You don't need to know the technical name of a repair — just describe what you're dealing with in plain language.

Try searching by:

- **Symptom** — describe the problem, like `leaky faucet`, `peeling paint`, or `squeaky floor`
- **Room** — narrow your results to a specific space, like `bathroom`, `kitchen`, or `garage`
- **Material or technique** — search for `tile`, `drywall`, `caulk`, or `grout` to find related guides

As you type, DIYHomie surfaces the most relevant results based on your search history, saved projects, and location-based seasonal tips.

## Filter Your Results

After searching or browsing the full library, use the **Filter** panel to refine your results. Tap the filter icon in the top-right corner of the Projects screen to open your options.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="By Difficulty">
    Match the project to your confidence and skill level:

    - **Beginner** — No prior experience needed. Simple tools, straightforward steps.
    - **Intermediate** — Some hands-on experience helpful. May require basic power tools.
    - **Advanced** — Requires prior knowledge or comfort with more complex techniques.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="By Category">
    Browse projects organized by trade or task type:

    - Plumbing
    - Electrical
    - Painting & Finishing
    - Flooring
    - Carpentry & Woodwork
    - Landscaping & Outdoor
    - HVAC & Ventilation
    - Appliance Repair
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="By Time Estimate">
    Filter by how much time you have available:

    - **Under 1 hour** — Quick fixes and small repairs
    - **Half day** — 2–4 hours of focused work
    - **Full day** — A longer single-session project
    - **Weekend** — Multi-step projects that span two or more days
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="By Room">
    Find projects scoped to a specific area of your home:

    - Kitchen
    - Bathroom
    - Living Room
    - Bedroom
    - Basement
    - Garage
    - Outdoor / Yard
    - Laundry Room
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

You can apply multiple filters at once. Active filters appear as tags below the search bar — tap any tag to remove it and broaden your results.

## Featured Collections

Not sure where to start? The **Featured Collections** section on the Projects home screen groups projects into curated themes — think *First-Time Homeowner Essentials*, *Winter Prep Checklist*, or *Budget Bathroom Refresh*. Collections are updated seasonally and highlight the most popular projects for each time of year.

Scroll horizontally on the Projects screen to browse active collections, or tap **See All Collections** to view the full list.

<Tip>
  Not sure what you need? Ask the AI assistant directly. Describe your problem in your own words — something like *"my sink is draining slowly"* or *"there's a crack above my door frame"* — and it will suggest the most relevant projects and explain what might be causing the issue.
</Tip>
