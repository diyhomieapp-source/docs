Life doesn't always let you finish a project in one sitting — and DIYHomie is built for that reality. Your progress is saved automatically as you work, so you never lose your place, and every project you've started or completed is tracked in one central dashboard.

## How Auto-Save Works

DIYHomie saves your progress every time you complete a step and advance in step-by-step mode. You don't need to tap a save button or stay connected to the internet — progress is written locally to your device first, then synced to your account as soon as a connection is available.

This means:

- **Closing the app mid-project** won't lose your place
- **Losing internet connection** while working doesn't interrupt anything
- **Switching devices** mid-project is safe — your progress will be waiting on the other device once it syncs

## My Projects Dashboard

The **My Projects** section (accessible from the bottom navigation bar) is your home base for everything you're working on. Projects are organized into three sections:

- **In Progress** — Active projects you've started but haven't finished. Shows the last step you completed and the percentage done.
- **Paused** — Projects you've explicitly paused or haven't touched in more than 7 days. These are surfaced separately so they don't get buried.
- **Completed** — A full history of every project you've finished, with your completion date and any photos you attached along the way.

## Progress Indicators

For each project in your dashboard, you'll see at a glance:

- **Step count** — e.g., *Step 4 of 11*, so you know exactly where you are in the process
- **Percentage complete** — A visual progress bar and numeric percentage
- **Time spent** — Total active time logged across all sessions for that project

These indicators update in real time as you complete steps.

## How to Resume a Paused Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open My Projects">
    Tap **My Projects** in the bottom navigation bar. Paused projects appear in the **Paused** section near the top of the screen.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the project">
    Tap the project card you want to continue. The detail page opens, showing your current progress and the last step you completed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap Resume Project">
    Tap the **Resume Project** button to jump directly back into step-by-step mode, starting at the next incomplete step. All your previous checkboxes and attached photos will be exactly as you left them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Marking a Project Complete

When you check off the final step of a project, DIYHomie prompts you to **Mark as Complete**. Confirming this action:

- Moves the project from **In Progress** to your **Completed** history
- Logs your finish date and total time spent
- Invites you to rate the project and leave notes for your own reference
- Unlocks a shareable summary card you can post or save as a keepsake

Completed projects stay in your history indefinitely. You can re-open any completed project at any time to review the steps, revisit your photos, or use it as a reference for a future project.
