Not every great project is one you're ready to start today — and that's exactly what Favorites are for. DIYHomie lets you bookmark any project in the library so you can build a personal list of ideas, plan ahead for upcoming renovations, and keep the guides you love just a tap away.

## How to Save a Project

You can bookmark a project from almost anywhere in the app:

- **From a project card** — Tap the bookmark icon in the top-right corner of any project card in the browse or search results view
- **From a project detail page** — Tap the bookmark icon in the page header, next to the project title
- **From step-by-step mode** — Tap the **⋯** menu in the top-right corner and select **Save to Favorites**

A filled bookmark icon confirms the project has been saved. Tap it again at any time to remove the project from your favorites.

## Accessing Your Saved Projects

All your bookmarked projects live in one place:

1. Tap your **Profile** icon in the bottom navigation bar
2. Select **Saved Projects**

From here you'll see every project you've bookmarked, sorted by the date you saved it (most recent first). You can switch to alphabetical or category order using the sort menu in the top-right corner.

## Organizing with Collections

Collections let you group saved projects into named lists so your favorites stay organized as the list grows. Create a collection for an upcoming renovation, a seasonal to-do list, or any theme that makes sense for how you plan your projects.

**Create a collection:**
- In **Saved Projects**, tap **New Collection**
- Enter a name (e.g., *Bathroom Reno*, *Spring Projects*, *Things Dad Showed Me*)
- Tap **Create** — your new collection is ready to fill

**Add projects to a collection:**
- Long-press any saved project card and tap **Add to Collection**, then choose the collection
- Or open a project detail page, tap the bookmark icon dropdown, and select a collection from the list

**Rename or delete a collection:**
- In **Saved Projects**, tap **Collections** to see all your collections
- Long-press a collection name to reveal **Rename** and **Delete** options
- Deleting a collection does not remove the bookmarked projects — they return to your main Saved Projects list

## Create a Collection and Add Projects

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Saved Projects">
    Go to **Profile → Saved Projects**. If you haven't created a collection yet, you'll see all your bookmarked projects in a single flat list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a new collection">
    Tap **New Collection** in the top-right corner. Type a name for your collection — something descriptive like *Kitchen Refresh* or *Weekend Fixes* — and tap **Create**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add projects to the collection">
    Long-press any project card in your saved list. Tap **Add to Collection** from the menu that appears, then select the collection you just created. Repeat for as many projects as you'd like to organize.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the collection anytime">
    Tap **Collections** at the top of the Saved Projects screen to switch to collection view. Select your new collection to see only the projects inside it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Get into the habit of bookmarking a project before you start it. That way, even if you navigate away during prep — to check the shopping list or ask the AI assistant a question — you can always find your way back to it instantly from Saved Projects.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Your favorites and collections sync automatically across all devices signed into your DIYHomie account. Save a project on your phone while browsing the store, and it'll be waiting on your tablet when you're ready to start at home.
</Note>
