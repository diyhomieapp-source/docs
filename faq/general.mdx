Whether you're new to DIYHomie or a regular user, you probably have questions about how the app works, what's included, and how to get the most out of it. We've collected the most common questions from our community and answered them here. If you don't find what you're looking for, try asking the DIYHomie AI assistant — or reach out to our support team at [support@diyhomie.com](mailto:support@diyhomie.com).

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is DIYHomie free to use?">
    DIYHomie offers a free plan that gives you access to a core selection of project guides, basic AI assistant interactions (up to 10 per month), standard shopping list generation, and seasonal maintenance tips. For unlimited AI access, the full project library, the Cost Estimator with regional pricing, offline downloads, PDF export, and priority support, the DIYHomie Premium plan is available as a monthly or annual subscription.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What platforms is DIYHomie available on?">
    DIYHomie is available on iOS (iPhone and iPad, iOS 15 and later), Android (Android 8.0 and later), and as a full-featured web app at app.diyhomie.com. Your account, project library, saved guides, and progress sync automatically across all platforms when you're signed in. The web app is fully responsive and works well on tablets, laptops, and desktop computers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many projects are in the library?">
    The DIYHomie library currently includes over 1,000 step-by-step project guides spanning interior repairs, kitchen and bathroom upgrades, outdoor and landscaping projects, painting, flooring, basic electrical, plumbing fixtures, and seasonal home maintenance. New guides are added weekly by our editorial team, and users can request specific projects through the app (see "Can I submit a project idea?" below). Free plan users have access to the core project library; Premium subscribers have unlimited access to the entire library, including advanced and specialty guides.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the AI assistant work?">
    The DIYHomie AI assistant is trained on our entire project library, safety guidelines, tool and materials knowledge base, and general home improvement expertise. You can ask it questions in plain language — "How do I fix a running toilet?", "What's the best paint for bathroom walls?", "Is this job safe for a beginner?" — and it will guide you with accurate, specific answers grounded in our library content. The AI assistant can also walk you through projects step-by-step, help you troubleshoot problems mid-project, and suggest what tools and materials you'll need. Free plan users receive up to 10 AI assistant interactions per month; Premium subscribers have unlimited access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use DIYHomie offline?">
    Yes — with a Premium subscription, you can download individual project guides for offline access. Downloaded guides include all steps, photos, tool lists, and materials lists, and are fully accessible without an internet connection. This is especially useful when you're working in a garage, basement, or outdoor area with limited connectivity. To download a guide, open it and tap the download icon in the top right corner. Offline downloads are synced automatically when you reconnect to the internet, so any progress you log is preserved.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I sync my progress between devices?">
    Your progress syncs automatically to your DIYHomie account as long as you're signed in on each device and have an internet connection. If you complete steps on one device while offline, your progress will sync the next time that device connects to the internet. If you notice your progress isn't syncing, see the [Troubleshooting FAQ](/faq/troubleshooting) for steps to resolve common sync issues.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What skill level do I need to use DIYHomie?">
    DIYHomie is designed for all skill levels — from complete beginners who have never used a drill to experienced homeowners tackling advanced renovation projects. Every project in the library is tagged with a difficulty rating (Beginner, Beginner to Intermediate, Intermediate, Intermediate to Advanced, or Advanced) so you can filter for projects that match your current abilities. We recommend starting with Beginner projects to build your skills and confidence before progressing to more complex work. The AI assistant can also assess any project for your skill level if you describe your experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I submit a project idea or request a specific guide?">
    Absolutely — we love hearing what projects our community wants to tackle. To submit a project request, go to your Profile tab and tap **Suggest a Project**. Describe the project you'd like to see covered, and our editorial team reviews all submissions weekly. Popular requests are prioritized and typically appear in the library within four to six weeks. You'll receive an in-app notification when a guide you requested is published.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does DIYHomie cover commercial or rental property projects?">
    DIYHomie guides are written for homeowners performing work on their primary or secondary residences. Most of the project content is equally applicable to rental properties, but we recommend always verifying local regulations before starting any work on a rental property — landlord DIY work is subject to different rules in some jurisdictions, and tenant-occupied properties have additional safety and notification requirements. We do not currently cover commercial building projects.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I update my skill level?">
    Your skill level helps DIYHomie personalize project recommendations and AI assistant responses. To update it, go to your **Profile** tab, tap **Edit Profile**, and choose your skill level — **Beginner**, **Intermediate**, or **Advanced**. Your setting takes effect immediately — project recommendations and difficulty filters update right away.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my personal data safe?">
    Yes. DIYHomie takes data privacy seriously. We collect only the information needed to provide and improve the service — your account details, project progress, saved guides, and app usage data. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. You can request a full export of your data or deletion of your account at any time from **Profile → Account Settings → Privacy**. For full details, review our [Privacy Policy](https://diyhomie.com/privacy).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    You can reach our support team in several ways:
    
    - **Email:** [support@diyhomie.com](mailto:support@diyhomie.com) — responses within one business day
    - **In-app:** Go to **Profile → Help & Feedback** to submit a request or report an issue directly from the app
    - **Live chat:** Available to Premium subscribers through the web app during business hours (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM ET)
    
    For technical issues, please include your device type, operating system version, and app version number (found in **Profile → About**) in your message — it helps us resolve your issue faster.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
