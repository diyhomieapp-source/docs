The DIYHomie AI Assistant is a conversational home improvement expert built directly into the app. Trained on thousands of repair guides, building codes, material specifications, and professional trade knowledge, it helps you diagnose problems, plan projects, choose the right tools and materials, and know when a job is better left to a licensed pro. Whether you're standing in front of a leaking pipe at midnight or trying to figure out what primer to buy before a weekend paint job, the AI assistant is ready to help the moment you need it.

## How to Access the AI Assistant

You can reach the AI assistant from anywhere in the app using any of these three entry points:

- **Floating chat button** — The speech-bubble icon in the bottom-right corner of every screen opens the assistant instantly, no matter where you are in the app.
- **Chat tab** — Tap the **Chat** icon in the bottom navigation bar to open the dedicated AI assistant screen, where you can browse your conversation history and start new threads.
- **Project page chat button** — Every project guide has a **"Ask AI"** button near the top of the page. Tapping it opens the assistant pre-loaded with context about that specific project, so you don't have to explain what you're working on.

<Note>
  The DIYHomie AI Assistant is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week on iOS, Android, and the web app — no subscription upgrade required.
</Note>

<Warning>
  AI responses are guidance only and do not constitute professional advice. Always follow your local building codes, safety regulations, and manufacturer instructions. When in doubt, consult a licensed contractor or tradesperson.
</Warning>
