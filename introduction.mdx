DIYHomie is an AI-powered home improvement companion designed for homeowners of every skill level — from first-time renters fixing a squeaky door to seasoned DIYers taking on full bathroom remodels. The app walks you through every project with clear, sequential instructions, curated tools and materials lists, and an AI assistant that answers your questions in plain language so you never feel stuck. This documentation covers everything you need to know to get started, navigate the app confidently, and get the best results from every feature DIYHomie offers.

## What can DIYHomie help you with?

DIYHomie covers a wide range of home repair, maintenance, and improvement projects, including:

- **Plumbing** — fixing leaks, unclogging drains, replacing faucets and fixtures, and installing shut-off valves
- **Electrical** — swapping outlets and switches, installing ceiling fans and light fixtures, and troubleshooting circuit breakers
- **Painting & Finishing** — interior and exterior painting, patching drywall, and applying trim and molding
- **Flooring** — laying tile, installing laminate or hardwood, and repairing subfloor damage
- **Appliance Repair** — diagnosing and fixing common issues with washers, dryers, dishwashers, and refrigerators
- **Outdoor & Landscaping** — deck maintenance, fence repair, gutter cleaning, and basic irrigation
- **Renovations** — kitchen and bathroom updates, basement finishing, and room additions

Whether you're tackling a ten-minute fix or a weekend-long renovation, DIYHomie scales with the project.

<Tip>
  Not sure where to start? Open the AI assistant and describe the problem you're facing in plain language — for example, "my bathroom faucet drips when it's off." The assistant will identify the most likely cause and point you to the right guide automatically.
</Tip>
