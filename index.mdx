## Get up and running in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download or Open the App">
    Install DIYHomie from the App Store or Google Play, or head straight to [app.diyhomie.com](https://app.diyhomie.com) in your browser — no installation required on the web.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up with your email address or continue with Google or Apple. Your free account saves your project history, tracks your progress, and personalizes your recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a Project">
    Search by symptom ("water under sink"), room ("bathroom"), or task ("install ceiling fan") to find the right guide. DIYHomie surfaces the most relevant projects for your skill level automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow Along">
    Work through each step at your own pace. Check off tasks as you complete them, view photos and diagrams, and tap the AI assistant button any time you need extra guidance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
